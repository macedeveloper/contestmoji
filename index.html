Mace Dev
Hey, ended up staying late at the studio again

Not super productive , but at least I got the layout done

I’m kind of in that weird headspace — not upset, just... drained

Might take a break tomorrow and just walk around the city or something.

Still debating if I want to bring the camera with me.

Anyway, hope your day went better than mine

Usage:

  1. Insert into the <head> of your website: <link rel="stylesheet" href="https://macestudios.ru/contestmoji/emojis.css">
  2. Anywhere on your website write: <i class="md-e md-e-smile"></i>
  3. Replace smile with your desired emoji name
