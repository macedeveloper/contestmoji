Contestmoji

Mace Dev

Example:

I feel rage

This is amazing

Let's party

Usage:

  1. Insert into the <head> of your website: <link rel="stylesheet" href="https://macestudios.ru/contestmoji/emojis.css">
  2. Anywhere on your website write: <i class="md-e md-e-NAME"></i>
  3. Replace NAME with the emoji name
